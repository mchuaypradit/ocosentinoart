QUOTA

is a series of mixed-media paintings, in which every collaged work incorporates numerous NYC parking tickets. As quickly as the ever-discarded orange and white paper rectangles are made trash to the NYC streets by their intended owners, they’re swept up by the artist and re-purposed as a unique textural element in a fleeting city scene.
